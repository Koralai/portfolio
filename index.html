About

Thank you for stopping by! My name is Carrie, and I created this portfolio to showcase the results of my experiments in web development.

Here's the long version, for anyone who's interested:

Over a decade ago, I was a suburban preteen with a flair for the dramatic and more than my share of spare time and adolescent angst, and I channeled these resources into making grunge-inspired websites that are still available, in somewhat mutilated form, on the internet Wayback Machine (props to anyone who can track them down!). As the years passed, life and other pursuits came between me and my budding passion for web development, but I never lost the interest in design, form, and functionality that those early experiments had instilled in me. I went on to study English and foreign languages and, more recently, to begin crocheting and knitting in my free time; and although these fields are not directly related to websites or technology, they involved me in a similar skillset of pattern recognition and integrated use of the logical/analytical and creative hemispheres of the brain.

Now, once again, I have the time and inclination to immerse myself in web development. There is a lot to learn, and for now this is just a hobby for me, but at the same time I know that I am capable of deep concentration and of learning efficiently. I'm excited to see how far this path takes me, and I hope you enjoy looking over these traces of the steps I have taken so far!
